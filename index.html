About

What is a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is an event to provide professional resources and a fun environment where students can create a project. Working as a team or as an individual, you will have 24 hours to execute the programming or electrical engineering project of your imagination. The day will include all kinds of fun, such as food, games, prizes, and also staying up all night!

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the location of Idahack?

Idahack will be taking place at ISU in Pocatello, ID. We will be using their Student Union Building. The address is:1065 Cesar Chavez, Pocatello, ID 83209

How much does it cost to attend?

Hackathons are completely free. The only cost would be getting yourself to the event.

What do I Need to Bring?

Bring all of your supplies that you will need to do your project. This is undoubtedly a computer but could be more if you were planning on doing a project that included some circuitry.

What else will be happening other than programming?

While developing a project will be the main goal, there will be games, speakers, and companies you can meet with during the night. This will be a great opportunity to network with companies that come out to see you and your projects. You can even get a project idea from some of these companies that come.

Do I have to stay up all night?

While we want you to be able to put the most time into your project, we cannot and will not force you to be awake. On a side note, it might be hard to sleep with all the caffeine that will be available to you.

How do I pick a project?

Your project should be something you are very interested in learning or are skilled in. This could be anything: implementing a neural network to recognize faces, making an attendance system, creating an Android app that will track steps. The sky is the limit!

How do I form a team?

Team forming will be happening on our slack channel. We will be making a team forming a channel where people can post projects they are interested in working on and you will be able to see what projects you could contribute to. The slack channel is https://idahackit.slack.com We will also have a small team forming event at the Hackathon. We would suggest getting to know and posting about your projects beforehand on the slack channel. Just a side note, you do not need to tell us what team you will be on.

This sounds great! Where do I register?

If you you'd like to be a participant, you can go here and fill out your information so we can plan on you coming! We will need volunteers helping out the day of the event, we will have a separate form for those that would like to volunteer.

Have any more questions?

Either send us an email at idahackit@gmail.com or connect with us on Slack and post it on there! We'd love to answer your questions.
